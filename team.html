max kanpp

Max Knapp values the independent, diligent, and forward-looking perspective that are the foundations of a truly strong planning pro cess. A listener first and talker second, Max is focused on building client relationships based on trust and understanding While he brings a broad knowledge base around portfolio construction and investment acumen, he is driven by learning about what is important to his clients, whether that's prepping for retirement or creating a lasting legacy.

Max started Blackbear Capital because of his desire to act as a fidu ciary - always putting our clients first as oppose to the myriad of stakeholders at a public company. As an entrepreneur himself, he can relate to our clients in a unique way and has a strong apprecia tion for an independent, employee-owned firm. Max understands that clients value competence and independent, conflict-free advice, not sales experience.

Max co-founded Blackbear Capital after spending nearly five years with United Bank's Wealth Management group. Whildet United. Max was responsible for managing assets for a wide variety of cli ents, including high net worth individuals, nonprofit institutions, pen sion plans, and retirees. Prior to joining United Bank, Max spent time in West Palm Beach as an Investment Associate with the private equity firm. Comvest Partners. He began his career on Wall Street with Deutsche Bank Securities where he developed a strong founda tion in financial statement analysis and business valuation

Outside of work, Max enjoys playing an active role in the community engaging in volunteer organizations as well as the growing entrepre neurship community throughout West Virginia. Max also enjoys spending time with his wife, Kylie, travelling and hiking local trails as well as spending time on the golf course. Max is an avid sports fan and can often be found cheering on the mountaineers.

EDUCATION
